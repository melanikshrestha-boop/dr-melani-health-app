# Privacy Policy - Dr. Melani Health App

**Last Updated:** June 19, 2026

## Introduction

Dr. Melani Health App ("we," "us," "our," or "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### From Whoop Integration
When you connect your Whoop account to our app, we access:
- Sleep data (duration, quality, recovery metrics)
- Health metrics and recovery scores
- Fitness and activity data

### Information You Provide
- Email address
- Health and wellness logs
- Personal notes and observations

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Display and track your health metrics
- Provide personalized health insights
- Improve your health app experience
- Sync data across your devices

## Data Security

Your data is stored securely and only accessible to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information with third parties without your explicit consent.

## Data Retention

You can delete your health data at any time. Data is retained as long as you use the app, unless you request deletion.

## Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Whoop to provide health data. Please review Whoop's privacy policy at https://www.whoop.com/privacy for their data practices.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of data collection
- Request information about how your data is used
